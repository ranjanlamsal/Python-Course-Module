# Session 2 (Friday) — Operators & Branching Logic, Iteration Essentials

## What we're covering today

1. **[Module 3 — Operators & Branching Logic](./03-operators-and-branching.md)**
   Arithmetic operators (including the two everyone finds confusing at first: `//` and `%`), comparison operators, logical operators, and `if`/`elif`/`else`.
2. **[Module 4 — Iteration Essentials](./04-iteration-essentials.md)**
   `while` loops, `for` loops with `range()`, and controlling loops with `break`, `continue`, and `pass`.

## Why today matters more than it looks

Every project for the rest of this course — starting with this weekend's ATM Simulator — is built from exactly two moves repeated in combination: **"decide what to do" (branching)** and **"do it more than once" (iteration)**. Today is where both are introduced. Everything after this session is really just new *data* to branch on and iterate over.
